Odd Pages

Odd Pages is a quintet that plays groovy alternative rock where they mix genres and time signatures wildely, and the result is a uniqe sound. They deliver the unexpeced, political lyrics, melancholia, happiness and vocal harmony. Odd Pages are based in Uppsala and have been playing together since November 2013.
